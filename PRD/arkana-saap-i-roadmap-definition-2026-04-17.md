## Strategic Position Dashboard

### MVP

- **Percentile Position Calculation** [basic]: Computes the owner's percentile rank for each tracked metric against the matched peer cohort using a batch calculation triggered at data ingestion. Covers 6–8 core financial ratios (gross margin, overhead ratio, revenue per employee, labor cost ratio, etc.). Recalculates on a monthly cadence.
- **Quartile Position Display** [basic]: Presents each metric as a named quartile position (top 25%, second quartile, third quartile, bottom 25%) alongside the exact percentile number. Uses a simple color-coded visual so the owner reads comparative standing at a glance rather than interpreting raw numbers.
- **Category-Based Layout** [basic]: Organizes available metrics into four standard categories (financial performance, cost structure, revenue dynamics, structural indicators) so the owner can scan by domain. Uses a fixed grid layout with collapsible category sections.
- **Sector Profile Configuration** [basic]: Captures the owner's industry, business size band, and primary operating geography at onboarding or via a settings update to calibrate briefing content. Uses the same segmentation dimensions as the Peer Position Engine for consistency.
- **Observation Generation** [basic]: Produces two to four discrete, numbered observations per briefing, each linking a specific intelligence finding to a concrete business implication or decision point. Observations are generated from templates calibrated to the owner's sector and metric positions.
- **Plain-Language Translation** [basic]: Rewrites all briefing findings in non-analyst vocabulary. Every insight is framed as a concrete business implication rather than a market observation. Uses template-guided language simplification to eliminate jargon.
- **Action Specificity Framing** [basic]: Frames each observation as a specific action the owner can evaluate (e.g., 'review your supplier contract terms before the Q3 cost increase takes effect') rather than a vague recommendation. Uses a structured action-verb + context + timeframe template.

### Increment 2 — Continuous Monitoring

- **Peer Cohort Summary Statistics** [basic]: Shows the cohort median, interquartile range, and top-decile threshold for each metric, giving the owner the full distribution context behind their position — not just where they sit, but what the landscape looks like around them.
- **Historical Position Tracking** [basic]: Maintains a rolling time-series of the owner's percentile position for each metric, displayed as a simple line chart showing trajectory (improving, stable, deteriorating) over the last 2–4 quarters. Updates monthly as new data flows in.
- **Threshold-Based Drift Detection** [basic]: Triggers an alert when any tracked metric's percentile position moves beyond a fixed default threshold (10 percentile points) over a rolling two-quarter window. Detection runs on a monthly batch cycle. Alerts are binary — threshold crossed or not.
- **Plain-Language Alert Generation** [basic]: Produces a human-readable alert statement (e.g., 'Your labor cost ratio moved from the 34th to 58th percentile over the last two quarters') using a template-based system. No jargon or statistical notation. Each alert states what moved and by how much.
- **Alert Delivery Channels** [basic]: Sends shift alerts via email and in-dashboard notification. Owner can choose between immediate delivery and a weekly summary digest. No granular per-metric frequency control yet.
- **Alert History Log** [basic]: Maintains a chronological list of all triggered alerts with their original text and the date triggered. Owner can scroll through past alerts to review the sequence of position shifts. No filtering or search yet.
- **Time Window Configuration** [basic]: Lets the owner set the rolling time window for trend display from three preset options (last quarter, trailing 6 months, trailing 12 months) applied globally across all dashboard metrics.
- **Display Mode Toggle** [basic]: Offers two display modes per metric — percentile rank or quartile band position — switchable with a single click on the dashboard. Trend chart view is not yet available.
- **Cohort Segmentation Matching** [advanced]: Adds sub-industry refinement within NACE codes and tighter size band granularity (e.g., 10–25 vs 25–50 employees). Supports automatic cohort reassignment when the owner's business crosses a size threshold. Flags when a cohort has fewer than the minimum participant count for statistical validity.
- **Percentile Position Calculation** [advanced]: Moves to a weekly recalculation cadence. Adds weighted recency scoring so more recent cohort data contributes more to the distribution. Expands tracked metrics from 6–8 to 12–15 covering all four metric categories.

### Increment 3 — Sector Intelligence & Actionability

- **Monthly Briefing Generation** [basic]: Produces a structured two-to-three-page briefing each month covering the top sector changes and their business implications. Assembled primarily from ČS sector research reports and public regulatory monitoring. Content is curated and edited by the system with human review in the loop.
- **Multi-Format Delivery** [basic]: Delivers each briefing via email, a web-readable view in the dashboard, and a downloadable PDF suitable for sharing with an accountant or business partner.
- **Evidence Linking** [basic]: Ties each observation back to the specific briefing section (sector change, peer benchmark, cost signal) that supports it via a clickable reference, so the owner can trace the reasoning behind each recommendation.
- **Additional Customer Information Gatherer** [basic]: At key moments (post-first-report, post-alert, monthly check-in), identifies the single highest-value data point not yet provided by the owner and prompts them to input it, offering a more detailed benchmark in return. Uses a static priority list of missing fields ranked by impact on benchmark accuracy.
- **Quartile Position Display** [advanced]: Adds peer distribution visualization — the owner sees not just their quartile but a histogram of where the cohort clusters, revealing whether the distribution is tight or spread. Adds contextual tooltips explaining what each quartile position typically implies for business health.
- **Cause Attribution Analysis** [basic]: Determines whether a position shift was driven by the owner's own metric changing, the peer cohort's distribution shifting, or both. States this explicitly in the alert using a rule-based comparison of owner delta vs cohort delta.
- **Bank Data Pre-Processing** [advanced]: Adds ML-based transaction categorization that improves accuracy over time. Derives additional metrics beyond the initial set (e.g., seasonality patterns, supplier concentration, payment timing). Supports incremental data refresh so the benchmark report updates as new transactions flow in.
- **Instant Position Summary** [advanced]: Expands from 4–6 core metrics to 10–12 metrics. Adds a comparison to the owner's own trajectory (if returning user) alongside peer comparison. Includes a 'what changed since your last report' section for returning users.
- **Cohort Segmentation Matching** [basic]: Assigns the owner to a peer cohort using NACE industry code, a broad employee/revenue size band, and Czech regional geography. Matching is rule-based with manual override if the owner's profile is ambiguous. Supports the minimum cohort granularity needed for segmented benchmarking — the table-stakes market standard.
- **Bank Data Pre-Processing** [basic]: Ingests consented ČS bank transaction data to derive key financial metrics (revenue, major cost categories, margin proxies) before the owner's first login. Uses rule-based transaction categorization to produce the initial metric set. Enables the pre-loaded report for The Bank-Referred Passive Adopter channel.
- **Manual Input Fast-Track** [basic]: Provides a structured onboarding form completable in under 15 minutes for owners without bank data integration. Collects enough financial data (revenue, headcount, top cost lines, margin) to generate an initial benchmark report within the same session. Serves The Exposed Owner who signs up directly.
- **Instant Position Summary** [basic]: Generates a one-page summary showing the owner's percentile position on 4–6 core metrics against their matched peer cohort, with a plain-language verdict for each (e.g., 'Your overhead ratio is higher than 72% of peers in your segment'). This is the day-one proof-of-insight that must convert both segments within 14 days.
- **Proof-of-Insight Highlight** [basic]: Identifies and surfaces the single most significant finding from the initial benchmarking — the metric where the owner's position is most divergent from the cohort median — as the lead insight on the first-login screen. Designed to deliver immediate proof of value and trigger the 'I didn't know that' moment.
- **Metric Selection and Prioritization** [basic]: Allows the owner to choose which metrics appear on their primary dashboard view from the available library. Default configuration is pre-set based on the most common metrics for their cohort segment. Owner can reorder metrics manually. Satisfies the Customizable Dashboards table-stakes market standard.


### Increment 4 — Deep Intelligence & Personalization

- **Peer Cohort Summary Statistics** [advanced]: Adds trend-over-time for cohort statistics — the owner sees not just today's median and IQR but how the cohort distribution has shifted over the last 4 quarters. Adds a 'cohort health indicator' showing whether the peer group is collectively improving or deteriorating.
- **Historical Position Tracking** [advanced]: Extends time-series to multi-year view. Adds annotation capability — the owner can tag events on their timeline (e.g., 'hired 3 people,' 'lost key client') to correlate position shifts with business decisions. Adds cohort trajectory overlay so the owner sees their movement relative to the cohort's movement.
- **Additional Customer Information Gatherer** [advanced]: Uses a dynamic prioritization model that considers the owner's current benchmark gaps, their engagement patterns, and the marginal value of each missing data point. Contextualizes requests within the flow (e.g., after an alert about labor costs, asks for headcount breakdown). Offers progressively richer benchmark unlocks as more data is contributed.
- **Threshold-Based Drift Detection** [advanced]: Adds configurable thresholds per metric (the owner can set tighter sensitivity for metrics they care about most). Introduces rate-of-change detection — alerts not just on threshold crossing but on acceleration of drift. Adds multi-metric correlation alerts (e.g., 'your margin and revenue per employee are both declining simultaneously').
- **Plain-Language Alert Generation** [advanced]: Moves from template-based to LLM-generated alert narratives that incorporate the owner's specific business context (sector, recent events, related metrics). Adds a 'what this typically means' paragraph drawing on patterns observed across the cohort.
- **Cause Attribution Analysis** [advanced]: Adds decomposition of cohort-side shifts — when the cohort moved, identifies whether it was a broad industry trend or a sub-segment shift. Links cause attribution to sector briefing content when a relevant briefing covered the same trend.
- **Alert Delivery Channels** [advanced]: Adds per-metric frequency control (e.g., immediate for margin metrics, weekly digest for structural indicators). Introduces priority-based alert ranking so the most significant shifts surface first. Adds mobile push notification channel.
- **Alert History Log** [advanced]: Adds filtering by metric, severity, time range, and cause type. Introduces pattern visualization — the owner can see which metrics trigger alerts most frequently. Adds the ability to mark alerts as 'acted on' or 'acknowledged' for personal tracking.
- **Forward-Look Identification** [basic]: Isolates two to three developments most likely to affect the owner's cost base, pricing environment, or competitive position over the next two quarters. Uses a rule-based relevance scoring model against the owner's sector profile. Provides estimated impact magnitude in qualitative terms (high/medium/low).
- **Urgency Calibration** [basic]: Tags each action observation with a time horizon indicator (immediate, next quarter, next six months) so the owner can prioritize which actions to evaluate first. Uses a rule-based mapping from the type of finding to typical decision timelines.
- **Accountant-Ready Formatting** [basic]: Structures each observation so it can be forwarded directly to the owner's accountant or advisor with sufficient context — includes the finding, the data source, the business implication, and the suggested action in a clean, professional format.
- **Metric Selection and Prioritization** [advanced]: Adds intelligent metric recommendations — the system suggests metrics the owner should monitor based on their sector, recent alerts, and peer patterns. Supports drag-and-drop reordering and metric grouping by custom themes.
- **Time Window Configuration** [advanced]: Adds custom date range selection per metric. Introduces comparison windows — the owner can overlay two time periods to see how their position changed between them. Adds fiscal year alignment option.
- **Display Mode Toggle** [advanced]: Adds the trend chart view showing position over time as a third display mode. Introduces a combined view that shows percentile rank with a sparkline trend in a compact format. Adds peer distribution overlay option.
- **Saved View Profiles** [basic]: Enables the owner to save multiple dashboard configurations (e.g., 'Monthly Review,' 'Pricing Prep,' 'Board Meeting') and switch between them with one click. Supports up to 5 saved profiles.
- **Manual Input Fast-Track** [advanced]: Adds intelligent form pre-fill using data already in the system. Reduces input time to under 8 minutes. Supports partial submission with progressive benchmark unlocking — the more data provided, the more metrics are benchmarked. Adds validation checks that flag implausible entries.
- **Proof-of-Insight Highlight** [advanced]: Identifies not just the most divergent metric but the most actionable divergence — weighing both magnitude and the owner's ability to influence the metric. Adds a 'what top-quartile peers do differently' teaser that motivates deeper engagement.

### Increment 5 — Predictive Maturity

- **Sector Profile Configuration** [advanced]: Adds multi-sector support for owners operating across industries. Introduces automatic profile refinement based on the owner's actual metric patterns (e.g., if their cost structure looks more like manufacturing than services, the system suggests a cohort adjustment). Adds competitor-type tagging so briefings can reference the owner's competitive context.
- **Monthly Briefing Generation** [advanced]: Adds automated web scraping of public market signals (procurement announcements, regulatory filings, trade publications) alongside ČS research. Briefings become personalized — content is weighted toward developments that affect the owner's specific metric positions. Reduces human review to exception-only.
- **Forward-Look Identification** [advanced]: Moves from rule-based to ML-based relevance scoring that learns from which forward-look items owners engage with. Adds quantitative impact estimates where data supports them (e.g., 'energy cost increases of 8–12% are expected to compress margins by 1.5–2 points for your cohort'). Links forward-look items to the owner's specific metric positions.
- **Plain-Language Translation** [advanced]: Uses LLM-powered rewriting calibrated to the owner's demonstrated reading level and engagement patterns. Adds contextual framing that connects sector-level findings to the owner's specific business situation. Supports Czech and English output.
- **Multi-Format Delivery** [advanced]: Adds branded PDF export suitable for board presentations. Introduces a shareable link with access controls so the owner can share specific briefings with advisors without giving full platform access. Adds calendar integration for briefing delivery scheduling.
- **Observation Generation** [advanced]: Observations now incorporate the owner's specific metric positions and recent alert history — not just sector-level findings but personalized implications. Uses LLM generation with retrieval from the owner's benchmark data and alert log. Adds cross-briefing continuity — references previous observations and whether the owner acted on them.
- **Action Specificity Framing** [advanced]: Adds estimated financial impact ranges where data supports them (e.g., 'renegotiating supplier terms could recover €X–Y based on peer cost structures'). Introduces conditional framing — 'if your Q3 revenue holds, then...; if it drops, then...' — for more nuanced guidance.
- **Evidence Linking** [advanced]: Adds multi-source evidence chains — each observation can reference both briefing content and the owner's benchmark data simultaneously. Introduces confidence indicators showing how strong the evidence base is for each recommendation.
- **Urgency Calibration** [advanced]: Uses ML-based urgency scoring that considers the owner's metric trajectory, the pace of sector change, and regulatory deadlines. Adds automatic re-prioritization — if a previously 'next quarter' item becomes urgent due to new data, it is escalated with an explanation.
- **Accountant-Ready Formatting** [advanced]: Adds direct export to common accounting software formats. Includes relevant benchmark data tables alongside the observation so the accountant can verify the analysis. Supports a 'advisor briefing pack' that bundles all current observations with supporting data.
- **Category-Based Layout** [advanced]: Adds custom category creation — the owner can define their own metric groupings beyond the four defaults. Introduces cross-category correlation highlights showing when metrics in different categories are moving together. Supports collapsible sub-categories for owners tracking many metrics.
- **Saved View Profiles** [advanced]: Adds unlimited saved profiles with tagging and search. Introduces scheduled profile activation — the 'Board Meeting' view auto-activates the day before a saved calendar event. Supports profile sharing with team members or advisors.
- **Bank Data Pre-Processing** [north_star]: Fully automated, near-real-time transaction processing with ML-based categorization that achieves >95% accuracy. Derives advanced metrics including cash flow patterns, supplier dependency scores, and seasonal revenue models. Supports multi-bank aggregation. Privacy-preserving architecture ensures client data never enters model training — the RAG foundation that enables the give-to-get network effect.
- **Manual Input Fast-Track** [north_star]: Under 5 minutes to complete with intelligent pre-fill from public registry data and previous inputs. Supports photo-upload of financial statements with OCR extraction. Provides real-time benchmark preview as the owner inputs each field — showing their position updating live as data enters. Full benchmark report generated before the owner finishes the form.
- **Instant Position Summary** [north_star]: Generates a comprehensive multi-page report covering all tracked metrics with narrative interpretation, peer comparison, trajectory analysis, and personalized action items — all before the owner's first click. Includes a predictive element showing where the owner's position is likely to move over the next quarter based on current trends. Adapts depth and focus based on the owner's segment (Exposed Owner vs Bank-Referred).
- **Proof-of-Insight Highlight** [north_star]: Identifies the insight with the highest combination of surprise value, actionability, and financial impact. Presents it with a 'what you can do about this' micro-action and a 'what top-quartile peers did' case pattern. Personalizes the framing based on the owner's entry point (pain event type for Exposed Owners, RM conversation context for Bank-Referred).

### North Star

- **Cohort Segmentation Matching** [north_star]: Fully dynamic cohort assignment using ML-based similarity matching that goes beyond NACE/size/geography to include business model type, growth stage, and operational profile. Supports multi-cohort comparison — the owner can benchmark against their primary cohort and an aspirational cohort simultaneously. Automatic cohort quality monitoring ensures statistical validity and alerts the owner when their cohort composition changes meaningfully.
- **Percentile Position Calculation** [north_star]: Near-real-time recalculation as new data enters the system. Covers 20+ metrics across all categories. Adds predictive percentile projection — shows where the owner's position is trending over the next 1–2 quarters based on their trajectory and cohort movement patterns. Incorporates confidence intervals reflecting cohort size and data freshness.
- **Quartile Position Display** [north_star]: Full interactive distribution visualization with the ability to explore peer clusters, see what separates quartile boundaries, and understand the behavioral patterns of top-quartile performers. Adds 'path to next quartile' guidance showing which metrics would need to change by how much to move up. Integrates with action recommendations.
- **Peer Cohort Summary Statistics** [north_star]: Full cohort intelligence layer showing multi-year distribution evolution, emerging sub-clusters within the cohort, and predictive cohort trajectory. Adds 'cohort insight' narratives explaining why the distribution is shifting (linked to sector briefing content). Supports drill-down into anonymized cohort segments to understand what drives top-performer separation.
- **Historical Position Tracking** [north_star]: Full multi-year time-series with event annotation, cohort overlay, and predictive trajectory projection. Adds pattern recognition — identifies recurring seasonal patterns, structural shifts, and inflection points in the owner's position history. Generates narrative summaries of the owner's positioning journey over time.
- **Additional Customer Information Gatherer** [north_star]: Fully autonomous data acquisition system that identifies the optimal moment, channel, and framing for each data request based on the owner's engagement patterns and the marginal value of each data point to their benchmark accuracy. Integrates with third-party data sources (with consent) to reduce manual input burden. The give-to-get loop is seamless — each contribution immediately unlocks a visible benchmark improvement, reinforcing the network effect.
- **Threshold-Based Drift Detection** [north_star]: Predictive drift detection that alerts before a threshold is crossed, based on trajectory analysis. Supports complex multi-metric alert conditions (e.g., 'alert me if margin drops AND revenue per employee drops simultaneously'). Learns from the owner's response patterns to calibrate sensitivity — reducing alert fatigue while ensuring critical shifts are never missed.
- **Cause Attribution Analysis** [north_star]: Full causal decomposition with sector-level, cohort-level, and owner-level attribution. Links position shifts to specific market events identified in sector briefings. Adds counterfactual analysis — 'if the market hadn't shifted, your position would be X' — to help the owner separate their own performance from external forces. Generates narrative cause explanations suitable for board or advisor discussions.
- **Plain-Language Alert Generation** [north_star]: Fully contextual, LLM-generated alert narratives that incorporate the owner's business context, recent actions, sector developments, and peer patterns. Each alert includes a 'what to consider' micro-recommendation. Tone and depth adapt to the owner's demonstrated preferences. Supports conversational follow-up — the owner can ask clarifying questions about any alert.
- **Alert Delivery Channels** [north_star]: Fully adaptive delivery system that learns the owner's preferred channel, timing, and frequency for different alert types. Supports all channels (email, in-dashboard, mobile push, SMS for critical alerts). Integrates with calendar to avoid alerting during meetings. Supports advisor forwarding — critical alerts can be auto-forwarded to the owner's accountant with context.
- **Alert History Log** [north_star]: Full alert intelligence layer with pattern analysis, trend visualization, and outcome tracking. Shows which alerts led to owner action and what the outcome was. Generates periodic 'alert review' summaries showing the owner's response patterns and suggesting sensitivity adjustments. Supports export for advisor review.
- **Sector Profile Configuration** [north_star]: Fully autonomous profile management that continuously refines the owner's sector profile based on their actual business behavior, metric patterns, and engagement signals. Supports complex multi-sector, multi-geography configurations for diversified businesses. Proactively suggests profile updates when the owner's business appears to be evolving.
- **Monthly Briefing Generation** [north_star]: Fully personalized, AI-generated briefings that synthesize ČS research, web-scraped signals, peer cohort movements, and the owner's specific metric positions into a unified narrative. Each briefing is unique to the owner. Supports on-demand briefing generation for ad-hoc strategic questions. Quality matches or exceeds analyst-produced sector reports.
- **Forward-Look Identification** [north_star]: Predictive intelligence layer that identifies emerging developments before they appear in mainstream sector reports. Uses pattern recognition across the full ČS client base to detect early signals (e.g., a cluster of firms in the cohort showing simultaneous cost increases suggests an upstream supply shift). Provides quantitative impact projections with confidence ranges and scenario variants.
- **Plain-Language Translation** [north_star]: Fully adaptive language engine that calibrates vocabulary, depth, and framing to each owner's demonstrated comprehension level and preferences. Supports real-time clarification — the owner can highlight any sentence and get a simpler or more detailed explanation. Maintains consistent voice and terminology across all platform outputs.
- **Multi-Format Delivery** [north_star]: Full omnichannel delivery with branded exports, interactive web views, mobile-optimized formats, and API access for integration with the owner's other tools. Supports collaborative viewing — the owner and their advisor can view and annotate the same briefing simultaneously. Automatic format optimization based on the recipient's device and context.
- **Observation Generation** [north_star]: Fully autonomous, AI-driven observation engine that synthesizes all platform intelligence — benchmarks, alerts, sector briefings, forward-looks, and the owner's action history — into prioritized, personalized strategic observations. Each observation is unique to the owner's situation. Supports multi-scenario observations ('if X happens, do Y; if Z happens, do W'). Learns from which observations the owner acts on to improve relevance over time.
- **Action Specificity Framing** [north_star]: Each action recommendation includes a specific implementation path, estimated financial impact with confidence range, relevant peer evidence, and a 'next step' the owner can take today. Supports conversational drill-down — the owner can ask 'how exactly would I do this?' and receive a detailed action plan. Integrates with the owner's calendar to suggest timing for action execution.
- **Evidence Linking** [north_star]: Full evidence graph connecting every recommendation to its complete chain of supporting data — from raw benchmark positions through sector trends to the specific logic that produced the recommendation. Supports interactive exploration of the evidence chain. Includes counter-evidence where it exists, building trust through transparency.
- **Urgency Calibration** [north_star]: Predictive urgency scoring that accounts for the owner's decision cycle, upcoming business events (contract renewals, fiscal year end, regulatory deadlines), and the rate of change in underlying signals. Automatically re-prioritizes the full action queue when new information arrives. Provides a 'strategic calendar' view showing all recommended actions plotted against their optimal timing windows.

Supports direct platform access for advisors with role-based permissions. Includes a 'discussion guide' that helps the owner structure the conversation with their advisor around the platform's findings.
- **Metric Selection and Prioritization** [north_star]: AI-driven dashboard curation that proactively surfaces the metrics most relevant to the owner's current situation — promoting metrics where position is shifting, where sector briefings indicate upcoming impact, or where peer patterns suggest attention is needed. The owner retains full control but the system does the prioritization work. Supports goal-linked metric tracking — metrics are organized around the owner's stated business objectives.
- **Time Window Configuration** [north_star]: Fully intelligent time window management that automatically adjusts display windows based on the metric's natural cycle (monthly for revenue, quarterly for structural ratios, annual for strategic indicators). Supports predictive time extension — showing projected positions into the future alongside historical data. Adds scenario comparison — 'what would my position look like if this trend continues for 2 more quarters?'
- **Display Mode Toggle** [north_star]: Full visualization suite including percentile rank, quartile band, trend chart, peer distribution, predictive projection, and narrative summary — all available per metric with intelligent defaults. Supports side-by-side metric comparison. Adds an 'executive summary' mode that reduces the entire dashboard to a single-screen strategic overview with the most important signals highlighted.
- **Category-Based Layout** [north_star]: Fully adaptive layout engine that reorganizes the dashboard based on the owner's current priorities, recent alerts, and sector developments. Supports custom categories, cross-category correlation views, and a 'focus mode' that temporarily elevates a single category to full-screen for deep analysis. Layout learns from usage patterns and adapts over time.
- **Saved View Profiles** [north_star]: Fully intelligent profile management with context-aware auto-activation, team sharing with role-based permissions, and AI-suggested profiles based on the owner's usage patterns and upcoming business events. Supports profile templates shared across the platform community (anonymized) so owners can adopt proven dashboard configurations from successful peers in their cohort.
